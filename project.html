The Pilot

THIS AUTUMN BLOQHOUSE WILL LAUNCH A FIRST PILOT TOGETHER WITH AN AIFM LICENSED REAL ESTATE FUND MANAGER

Certificates, which represent the economical ownership of a real estate fund, are put on the Ethereum blockchain. The real estate tokens (Bloqs) are tradeable completely peer-to-peer, no exchange nor a broker is required. Onboarding processes ensures that regulation is covered. The Bloqwallet Ðapp completely disrupts the trading ecosystem as it allows investors to manage and trade their real estate portfolio fully peer-to-peer.
